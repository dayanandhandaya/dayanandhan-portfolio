About me

Builder. Researcher. Community leader.

I'm Dayanandhan Sakthivel, a Senior Business Analyst and Product Owner at Accenture with 6+ years of experience building intelligent digital products, AI-driven platforms, and automation solutions. My work lives at the intersection of Product Management, Artificial Intelligence, and Business Strategy.

I currently lead AI and GenAI-based product initiatives — collaborating with stakeholders, engineering, and design teams to deliver high-impact solutions using Agile. I've designed and implemented GenAI-powered agents that streamline workflows, reduce manual effort by 30–40%, and support data-driven decision-making across teams.

More recently, I've been working on multi-agent AI architectures, building a Super Agentic Application that integrates multiple specialised AI agents into a unified platform — enabling complex tasks through coordinated, intelligent AI-driven workflows.

Alongside my professional work, I am pursuing a PhD in Product Management, researching Human–AI collaborative decision support for Agile backlog management and prioritisation. I also lead UI/UX initiatives using Figma and apply design thinking to ensure products are intuitive and scalable.
